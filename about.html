Our Story of Impact

Discover the journey, mission, and dedicated team behind Nitasema Foundation's work empowering children in Kenya

Our Core Purpose

Guiding principles that drive everything we do

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable children in Kenya through comprehensive education, healthcare, and emotional support programs that transform lives and build resilient communities.

Our Vision

A Kenya where every child, regardless of background, has equal access to quality education, healthcare, and the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped our foundation

Foundation Established

Founded by a group of Kenyan educators and healthcare professionals who witnessed the struggles of children in rural communities.

2017

First School Program

Launched our first educational initiative in Kibera, providing learning materials and after-school support to 200 children.

2018

Healthcare Expansion

Established mobile health clinics to serve remote communities, reaching over 5,000 children with essential healthcare services.

2019

National Recognition

Awarded the National Humanitarian Award for our innovative approach to child empowerment and community development.

2020

Digital Learning Initiative

Launched our digital literacy program, providing tablets and internet access to rural schools during the pandemic.

2022

Community Centers

Opened three new community centers offering comprehensive support services to children and families across Kenya.
