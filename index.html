Our Mission

Creating sustainable change through compassion and empowerment

Transforming Lives in Kenya

Founded in 2010, the Nitasema Foundation has been at the forefront of child and women empowerment initiatives across Kenya. We operate a home-based care program for vulnerable children and orphans, currently supporting over 15 children under our direct care.

Our holistic approach focuses on comprehensive support through education, healthcare, economic empowerment, and emotional wellbeing. We believe every child and woman deserves access to opportunities that allow them to thrive.

With transparency and accountability as our core principles, we ensure that every donation directly impacts those we serve. Our team of dedicated professionals and volunteers work tirelessly to create brighter futures.

Women Empowerment

Building stronger communities by empowering women and girls

Creating Opportunities for Women

Our women empowerment programs focus on economic independence, education, and leadership development. We provide vocational training, microloans, and business mentorship to help women achieve financial stability.

Through our mentorship programs, we connect young girls with successful women role models in various fields. We also conduct workshops on reproductive health, family planning, and women's rights.

Our safe spaces provide women with opportunities to network, share experiences, and access counseling services. We believe empowered women create empowered communities.
