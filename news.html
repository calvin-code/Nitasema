New Primary School Opens in Rural Kenya

We're proud to announce the opening of our newest primary school in the Kajiado region. The school features 6 classrooms, a library, and modern sanitation facilities. This initiative will provide education to over 200 children who previously had to walk 10km to the nearest school.

Mobile Medical Clinic Serves Remote Communities

Our mobile medical unit recently completed a two-week tour of remote villages in Turkana County, providing essential healthcare services to over 500 children and their families. The team administered vaccines, treated common illnesses, and provided nutrition education to combat malnutrition in the region.

Clean Water Project Reaches 10,000 Beneficiaries

Our community water initiative has now provided clean drinking water to over 10,000 residents across 5 villages. The project includes boreholes, water purification systems, and hygiene education. Access to clean water has reduced waterborne diseases by 75% in participating communities.

Annual Volunteer Appreciation Event Celebrates Dedication

We celebrated our incredible volunteers at our annual appreciation event in Nairobi.
